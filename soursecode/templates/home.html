Marvel Cinematic Universe

This universe is an American media franchise and shared universe that is centered on a series of superhero films, independently produced by Marvel Studios and based on characters that appear in American comic books published by Marvel Comics

The shared universe, much like the original Marvel Universe in comic books, was established by crossing over common plot elements, settings, cast, and characters.

Movies:

The Marvel Cinematic Universe is divided into four stages.The first film released in the MCU was Iron Man (2008), which began the first phase of films.The films are currently in Phase Three, which began with the release of Captain America: Civil War (2016).
